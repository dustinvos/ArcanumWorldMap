Oronoco

Location
Surrounded by lush rainforests and jungle. Three rivers flow around the city: Red River, Green River, and Oronoco River on the Grey Coast.
Climate
Hot and humid
Population
Majority human, elf and half-elf population with a higher than average tiefling population.
Rule
Ruled by Queen Aphra (human)
Exports
Exotic woods, gems, exotic hides
Notable Locations
Grand Temple of Sulis (life), Oronoco Zoological Society, The Pitch Pits, numerous jungle ruins
×

Tierney

Location
Rolling hills and grassland in the northern portion of the continent. The Pale river flows through the city.
Climate
Cool and wet climate.
Population
Large halfling and gnome population.
Rule
Ruled by a 12-person council of halflings, gnomes, dwarves, humans, and elves.
Exports
Sapphires, cold ore, gnomish trinkets, beasts of burden
Notable Locations
The Borrows Marketplace, Grande Temple of Reva (nature), Council Tower, Barrier Mountain Wall (defensive wall to prevent orc invasions) runs from Tierney to Snow Falls
×

Navaz

Location
City on the Grand Plateau on the Grey Coast and Ash Channel and backs up to the Tusk Mountains.
Climate
Temperate climate
Population
Large human and half-elf population
Rule
Ruled by a council of wealthy shipping guilds led by the West Weld Shipping Company, Baron Tillerson (half-elf)
Exports
Ships
Notable Locations
Grand Temple of Revo (tempest), Navez Naval Yards, Great Lighthouse of Navez

Yawata

Location
Located on the Crotoan Island. Mountainous, cool and wet.
Climate
Cool and wet
Population
Largest dragonborn population in all cities
Rule
Ruled by the priests of Tenjin (knowledge) and the chief priestess Eraina (elf)
Exports
Obsidian, books, mithril
Notable Locations
Grand Temple of Tenjin, Great Library of Yawata, Mount Crotoa (volcano), Elite Panarii Temple (all gods), crossing bridges

Amarna

Location
Located on the Iteru River and the Lir Sea. Other than the floodplains of the Iteru and its delta, Amarna is surrounded by desert.
Climate
Hot and humid climate
Population
Primarily human and elvish population
Rule
Ruled by a line of hereditary kings, currently Pharaoh Akhenaten VI
Exports
Gold, linen, silks, dyes
Notable Locations
Grand Temple of Belenus (light), Hall of Kings, The Great Pyramid of Amarna, The Aten Stone, The Grand Bazaar
×

Garolden

Location
Located on the Harvest River and Sanguine Ocean. Surrounded by dense forests.
Climate
Temperate climate
Population
All races are well represented in Garolden with a larger human population and very small dragonborn and tiefling population.
Rule
Garolden is a republic with each district of the city electing a member to the council. The council elects a chancellor to serve as executive leader, currently Chancellor Claudius (human).
Districts
Military, religious, commerce, agricultural, upper class, middle class, and racial districts (except half-orc, half-elf, tiefling, and dragonborn)
Exports
Weapons, armor, lumber
Notable Locations
Grand Temple of Enki (trickery), Official Panarii Temple (all gods), Chancellor's Tower, Pentangle Market, The Bard's College
×

Ravenmourne

Location
Southernmost city on the Claw Peninsula, On the Gold Sea surrounded by grassland plains.
Climate
Wet and temperate climate
Population
Ravenmourne is the ancestral home of the elves and is primarily populated by elves, but other races live here in smaller numbers.
Rule
Ruled by a council of 9 elders (600+ years old required)
Exports
Bows, art, literature, magic
Notable Locations
Mages colleges, Felled Forest, almost every building is centuries old and filled with magic, Grand Temple of Morrighan (death)
×

Snow Falls

Location
Snow Falls is the northern most city of Arcanum on the Kelvin River and Kalt Ocean. It is a cold city located among the Barrier Mountains and features several waterfalls.
Climate
Cold
Population
Snow Falls has a larger Halfling, gnome, and dwarven population and a significant drow population.
Rule
Ruled by a hierarchy count/countess at the head. Current ruled by Countess Sparklegem (gnome)
Exports
Gnomish gadgets, Underdark ores and treasures, diamonds
Notable Locations
Frozen Falls, Panarii Snow Temple (all gods), Barrier Mountain Wall, northern lights
×

Iron Terra

Location
Dwarven city in the Iron Mountains.
Climate
Cold above ground, warm below ground
Population
Ancestral home to dwarves. Few races allowed to live with the dwarves underground. Non-dwarves mostly live in the above ground areas near Mile High Market.
Rule
Ruled by council of 5 elders (all dwarves, 400 years old requirement)
Exports
Stone, gems, ores, all things dwarven
Notable Locations
Dwarven Council, Grand Temple of Neto (war) Magmin's Labyrinth, Deep Roads, Molten Forge, Mile High Market

Religions of Arcanum

Arcanum has a pantheon of 8 major gods and goddesses. Many minor gods are worshipped, but usually have only small, regional followers. The 8 Deities have temples all across the land, and one Grand Temple each.

Morrighan

Goddess of death

Represented by 3 ravens

Alignment: Neutral Evil

Grand Temple: Ravenmourne

Tenjin

God or goddess of knowledge

Represented by a flowering tree

Alignment: Lawful Evil, magic

Grand Temple: Yawata

Sulis

Goddess of life

Represented by 3 intersecting rings

Alignment: Neutral Good

Grand Temple: Oronoco

Belenus

God of light

Represented by the sun

Alignment: Lawful Neutral, time

Grand Temple: Amarna

Reva

Goddess of nature

Represented by 3 leaves

Alignment: Chaotic Neutral

Sister of Revo

Grand Temple: Tierney

Revo

God of tempest

Represented by an eye

Alignment: Chaotic Evil, wealth

Brother of Reva

Grand Temple: Navaz

Enki

God of trickery

Represented by a crescent moon

Alignment: Chaotic Good, thieves

Grand Temple: Garolden

Neto

God of war

Represented by a flaming anvil

Alignment: Lawful Good, warriors

Grand Temple: Iron Terra
